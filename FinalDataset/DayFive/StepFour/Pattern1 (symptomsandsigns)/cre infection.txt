

Symptoms of CRE infections vary with the infected organ system(s) (for example, kidney or flank pain with pyelonephritis, fevers, or pain and pus production in wound infections). Nevertheless, these symptoms can also be caused by non-CRE infections. There are no specific symptoms of CRE infections; however, there are problems that can develop that make physicians suspicious that CRE infections may be the cause of symptoms.
